Overview

Celebrate Onam with a soul-refreshing journey to the Eastern Himalayas! This 5-day holiday special takes you from Kochi to the serene hills of Sikkim and Darjeeling, with everything taken care of—from flights and hotels to meals and sightseeing. Enjoy a scenic drive through lush tea gardens, visit iconic spots like Nathula Pass, MG Marg, and Tiger Hill, and ride the charming Darjeeling Himalayan Railway Toy Train. This is more than a tour—it’s a celebration filled with culture, comfort, and memories. And yes—don’t miss the highlight: a traditional Onam Sadya lunch amidst the Himalayas!

Included/Exclude

  • Return Airfare (Kochi – Bagdogra – Kochi)

  • 4 Nights Stay – 2N Gangtok & 2N Darjeeling

  • All Meals – Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner (APAI)

  • Darjeeling Toy Train Ride & Onam Sadya

  • Personal Expenses (Tips, Shopping, etc.)

  • Entry Tickets to Monuments

  • Travel Insurance
